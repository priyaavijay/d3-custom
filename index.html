Marks and Channels
This visualization displays the global distribution of top Instagram influencers, represented by colored and sized bubbles. Each country is color-coded based on the normalized average influence score of its influencers, while individual influencers are shown as circles positioned around their respective countries when the country is clicked. The size of each circle reflects the influence score, the color intensity reflects follower count and the thickness of their borders reflect the number of posts they have made on instagram. Users can click on a country to zoom in and examine influencer details within that country and click anywhere else on the globe to zoom out of it.

**Category of Custom Visualization:** Combinatorial
Our visualization combines geospatial mapping with bubble chart to display multiple attributes per influencer, creating a unique and informative view of influencer data across the world.
